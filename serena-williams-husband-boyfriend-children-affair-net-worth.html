Who is Serena Williams?

Tennis star who won every significant tournament and became the oldest champion number one ever in 2013 is none other than Serena Williams. She has caught 23 singles titles to accompany her older sister Venus Williams with 14 doubles titles.

However, from her father and mother's former marriages, she has three step-siblings each. Dylan Starr Williams, Ronner Williams, Richard Williams, Yetunde Price (late), Isha Price, and Lyndrea Price. Williams was indeed home-schooled. 

At the age of 14, Serena started off her career professionally against the wish of her parent. During that time due to age issue, she joined her first professional tournament after some setbacks by the WTA. 

In 1999, she won her first major championship and finished the Grand Slam career in 2003. Serena has teamed up with sister Venus Williams to win a series of doubles titles together with her individual success. 

Most recently, she easily won over Russian tennis player, Maria Sharapova just in an hour, wearing a shortened black bodysuit. Her Bodysuit instantly became famous among all the tennis fans.
