Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jessica Stroup was born in Anderson, South Carolina, and spent her childhood in Charlotte, North Carolina.

After graduating from Providence High School in 2004, she received a full scholarship to the University of Georgia.

However, she chose to move to Los Angeles to pursue her acting career. Her first acting role was a guest appearance on the show Unfabulous in 2005, which kickstarted her journey in the entertainment industry.

Breakthrough Role in 90210

Stroup’s major breakthrough came when she was cast as Erin Silver in the reboot of 90210.

This role significantly boosted her career and finances. Playing a high school student and the younger sister of characters from the original Beverly Hills, 90210, Jessica became a household name.

The show’s success contributed greatly to her net worth, and in 2010, she won the Sparkling Performance award at the Young Hollywood Awards.

Film Career and Earnings
